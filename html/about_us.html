About CNR Farm Web App

CNRFarm is a farm record web application that runs on a web browser, utilized for smart and effective record keeping of livestock activities and enterprises.
This webapp is created through interlinked functionings of HTML, CSS, Firebase and JavaScript. Unlike physical book recordings, it stores and secures data
quality with strict privacy standards by firebase; a secure real-time database. It comprises three major livestock activities viz. Dairy, Piggery & Poultry, covering
wide range of features such as livestock censuses, production statistics, health statuses and livestock populace.

Vision


CNRFarm will strive to be the most versatile webapp utilized as a user-friendly digitalized e-farm record that can be used in every potential farm for effective
farming accountancy.

Mission


Offer various features in farm recording webapp for different farming systems which instills effective farm accountancy.
Serve as user friendly e-farm book with secured and portable data quality.
Promote smart farming through information technology amongst farmers and pre-empt livestock digitalization in Bhutan.

But Why CNRFarm WebApp?

It is an innovation and renovation of simpler farm record books into a virtually dynamic e-record, that allows effective accountancy of farm activities and enterprises.
Secured data of farm documents can be teleported from one computer to another through web link.
It widely monitors different farms at a time through easy data viewing.
