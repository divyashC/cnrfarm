About CNR Farm Web App

CNRFarm is a farm record web application that runs on a web browser, utilized for smart and effective record keeping of livestock activities and enterprises. This webapp is created through interlinked functionings of HTML, CSS, Firebase and JavaScript. Unlike physical book recordings, it stores and secures data quality with strict privacy standards by firebase; a secure real-time database. It comprises three major livestock activities viz. Dairy, Piggery & Poultry, covering wide range of features such as livestock censuses, production statistics, health statuses and livestock populace.
